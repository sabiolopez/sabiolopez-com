# The Architecture of Decisions

In the world of B2B SaaS, a button is rarely just a button. It's the culmination of technical constraints, business logic, and user intent.

## The Problem with Prettiness
Most designers focus on making the interface look clean. In complex systems—think ERPs or CRM portals—"clean" can sometimes mean "hiding the truth."

### Systemic Thinking
When we design for systems, we must map every decision. 
- **Input:** Raw data from user or system.
- **Logic:** What happens to that data?
- **Output:** The result that drives the user to the next step.

> Architecture is not just how it looks; it's how it works.
