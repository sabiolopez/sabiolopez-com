## The Problem with "Prettier Buttons"

Many SaaS companies fall into the trap of thinking UX is about making things look modern. But in a complex ERP or CRM, a "pretty" button doesn't help if the user doesn't understand why they are pressing it.

> "Decision architecture is about reducing the cognitive load of a process, not necessarily the number of steps."

## The Roles of AI in Modern Design

As we integrate LLMs and custom agents into our design workflows, our role shifts from creators to architects. We are no longer just drawing boxes; we are defining the boundaries of how data is synthesized and presented.

1.  **Context Ingestion:** How the system understands user intent.
2.  **Hypothesis Mapping:** Validating business goals through rapid prototyping.
3.  **Results Engineering:** Delivering performance-grade interfaces.
